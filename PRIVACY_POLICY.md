# Privacy Policy  
**Violin Lesson Tracker**

_Last updated: [Insert Date]_

Thank you for using **Violin Lesson Tracker** ("the App").  
This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when using the App.

---

## 1. Overview  
Violin Lesson Tracker is designed as a personal practice journal.  
The App does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third parties. All data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to delete it.

---

## 2. Information We Collect  
The App does **not** collect any personally identifiable information.

The data you enter—including lesson notes, practice topics, pieces, evaluation scores, and optional recording links—is stored **locally on your device**.

The App does **not** collect:
- Name, email, or contact information  
- Device identifiers  
- Location data  
- Usage analytics  
- Recording files  

---

## 3. Data Storage  
All lesson-related data is stored securely on your device using Apple’s local storage technologies (such as SwiftData or Core Data).  
No data is uploaded to a server operated by the developer.

If you delete the app, all stored data will be removed from your device.
